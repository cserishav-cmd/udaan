For Students: Your Personal Wellness Toolkit

AI First-Aid

Get immediate, 24/7 support and coping strategies from our empathetic AI companion.

Confidential Booking

Privately book appointments with on-campus counsellors or mental health helplines.

Peer Support Forum

Connect anonymously with trained student volunteers in a safe, moderated community.

Self-Assessments

Use standard screening tools like PHQ-9 and GAD-7 to track your mental well-being over time.

Wellness Journal

A private space to pen down your thoughts, track your mood, and reflect on your personal journey.

Mindful Activities

Engage in relaxing games, guided meditations, and calming music to de-stress and refocus.

For Institutions: Proactive Campus-Wide Support

Trend Analytics

View anonymized data on the admin dashboard to recognize mental health trends and plan effective interventions.

Easy Integration

Tailor the platform to your institution, mapping to offline support systems and reflecting local cultural contexts.

Content Moderation

Manage the peer support forum, handle reports, and ensure a safe environment for all student interactions.

Resource Management

Easily add, update, and manage the multilingual psychoeducational resources available to all students.

The Need for a Digital Platform

Most available apps are generic, Western-oriented, or paid. Udaan is a tailored, open-source solution built to address the specific needs of Indian students and institutions.

  • Integrates regional cultural context and language.
  • Allows institution-specific customization.
  • Maps to offline support like college counsellors.
  • Provides real-time, anonymized analytics for admin.
  • Completely confidential and secure by design.
